## Why this exists

There's something about putting words into the world that feels like planting a seed. You don't know exactly what it will grow into, but you know it needs soil, light, and time.

This is The Green Room — a place for writing, images, links, and whatever else needs a home. Think of it as a journal with the door left open.

> The things you own end up owning you. It's only after you lose everything that you're free to do anything.

## What to expect

Some posts will be long. Some will be short. Some will just be a photo and a sentence. The only rule is that it has to feel honest.

Here's what I'm interested in:

- How things are made
- Why certain ideas stick and others don't
- The intersection of design and feeling
- Music, always music

## A starting point

Every good thing starts somewhere unremarkable. This is that somewhere. Thanks for being here.
